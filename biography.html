Biography

I'm a psychology student in Philadelphia... and so much more.

Originally from central New Jersey, in 2012 I began my studies at Drexel University in Philadelphia. There I study psychology along with philosophy and biology. In addition to my studies, I am also a member of the Creativity Research Lab and the Nezu Stress and Coping Laboratory.

In January of 2014, some close friends and I started a student organization, the Drexel Undergraduate Research Council whose primary goal is to increase access to undergraduate research opportunities at the university. To achieve this goal we host talks and panels related to undergraduate research, as well as serve as a venue for an undergraduate researcher network, to directly connect students to research opportunities. As another venue to achieve this goal, we also formed The Dragon, Drexel's Undergraduate Research Journal, whose mission is to showcase the research of Drexel undergraduates across all disciplines.

In my spare time, I like to run, read, and take pictures.
